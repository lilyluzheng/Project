Want to learn to skate?

First things first when you want to learn to skateboard you want to start by doing 2 things, get a skateboard and do some research to give you an idea of what you will be learning, and once you've done that, you'll be able to hit the streets and begin learning.

What skateboard to buy?

When looking for your first skateboard you want to be looking for quality instead of the price. Good beginner skateboards can range from £30-£70 and more better quality ones can range from £80 or £90+ ! So as a beginner you would want a board that rolls well and is good enough to learn the basics.

To know more about skateboards themselves, they are made of many different parts of which you can pick and choose your own style and design. The skateboard is made out of the following parts:

  • Deck (Wooded part that you stand on, comes in various sizes (width) based on personal preference.)
  • Griptape (Sand paper like adhesive to allow a grip on the deck to be able to perform tricks.)
  • Trucks (The metal T-shaped pieces that mount onto the underside of the deck)
  • Bearings (The round metal pieces that fit inside the wheels to mount the wheels to the axle.)
  • Wheels(The part of your skateboard that allow you to move, and help determine how fast you can go.)
  • Bolts and nuts (Used to connect the skateboard trucks to the skateboard deck.)
